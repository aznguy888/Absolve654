Who runs Libre.fm?

Libre.fm is run by FooCorp, headed up by Matt Lee. There are many programmers of the Libre.fm website too, including Clint Adams, Michael Sheldon, Jorgen Bohnsdalen, Toby Inkster, Daniel Watkins, Tony Biondo, Carlos Perilla, John Sullivan, Evan Hanson, Nicolas Reynolds, Corey Farwell, Bernd Gruber, Marius Orcsik, David Mignot, Jarkko Piiroinen, Mikael Nordfeldth, Nikola Plejic, Rob Myers and Ward Vandewege.

The programmers work with FooCorp to produce the software that powers the site. This software is free software, or software libre — implying that the software is free in the sense of freedom, not price. This allows you to set up and run your own website, rather than use Libre.fm if you'd prefer. One reason you might want to do this, is for your own privacy.

If you have any questions for Matt Lee, you can send them to him directly, at mattl@libre.fm, alternatively, you can write to us for support and feedback at support@libre.fm.

If you think you've found a bug in our website software, you can send an email directly to the programmers, who will look into the problem and try and fix it. Their address is bugs@libre.fm.
